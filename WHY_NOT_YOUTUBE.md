# Why Not YouTube?

This document explains why wista-project explores alternatives.

---

## Problems We Observe

- Algorithmic opacity
- Incentive misalignment between creators and viewers
- Forced identity and accounts
- Over-centralization of discovery and delivery
- Lack of inspectable infrastructure

---

## What We Question

- Should search require platform permission?
- Should metadata be proprietary?
- Should playback be inseparable from the platform?
