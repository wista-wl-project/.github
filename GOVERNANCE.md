# Governance

This document describes how **wista-project** is governed.

---

## Project Ownership

wista-project is maintained by its core maintainers.
The project does not belong to any company or commercial entity.

---

## Decision Making

- Technical decisions are made by maintainers
- Community input is encouraged via Issues and PRs
- In case of disagreement, maintainers have final responsibility

Consensus is preferred, but not required.

---

## Scope & Direction

The project prioritizes:
- Architectural clarity
- Experimental freedom
- Modular design
- Long-term maintainability

Features that conflict with these goals may be declined.

---

## Forking Policy

Forks are explicitly allowed and encouraged.

If you disagree with the direction of the project,
forking is considered a valid and respected option.

---

## Project Continuity

There is no guarantee of long-term maintenance.
This project exists as long as maintainers choose to continue.
